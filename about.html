About Me

My Studies

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Sydney. Throughout my academic journey, I extensively studied software design and testing, database management, agile methodologies, artificial intelligence and data science. In 2020, I participated in the Microsoft Student Accelerator Program, where I engaged in a series of workshops and competitions. Notably, I achieved success as a winner in their second bootcamp, where I created a program leveraging Azure IOT Hub and Stream Analytics. This involved transmitting data to be visualised in Power BI, showcasing my proficiency in utilising Microsoft's cutting-edge technologies.

My Work

I have two years of experience at EY as a technology consultant. My initial assignment involved working as a data engineer at a prominent Australian insurance company. In this role, I utilised Python, Spark and SQL to develop complex data transformations and thoroughly test and validate the client's data. I collaborated with subject matter experts and team members to create data mappings and logic from source to target. Additionally, I facilitated Scrum activities and documented technical mappings to align with the client's business glossaries.

Later on, I transitioned to my second project, serving as a Technical Business Analyst at a Big 4 Australian bank. In this capacity, I facilitated workshops in collaboration with domain architects and business stakeholders to define requirements, and I refined and groomed backlogs in preparation for each sprint, ensuring clarity and effectiveness throughout the development process.

Beyond my project responsibilities, I actively participated in EY's Women in Tech group, where I organised and facilitated book club events. Additionally, I had the opportunity to co-facilitate a workshop focussed on Cloud technologies. As part of my commitment to continuous growth and learning, I pursued upskilling opportunities, expanding my knowledge in areas such as Kafka, Airflow, Azure and AWS. Notably, I obtained my Azure AZ-900 certification in Azure Fundamentals.

Upskilling and Beyond

In addition to my continuous growth at EY, I have been actively pursuing more upskilling opportunities by immersing myself in all things related to full-stack development. This includes diving into React, JavaScript, Express, and Node, and applying and honing my skills in these areas. I have successfully completed several projects that showcase my capabilities.
